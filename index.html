What is LOTTOFUND

LottoFund combines the power of crowdfunding and the lottery. It creates opportunities for you to realize your most treasured dreams, while helping other people do the same. It is a special kind of charity based on the belief that good deeds always pay off. It gives you a chance to help your neighbor, while attracting good luck in your life.

MORE
How it works? In order to gather the needed sum you are taking the next steps:
Sign up and start
Register on our website and start a fund-raising campaign for making your dream come true.
Invite friends
Tell the friends about your campaign in the social networks and invite them to participate.
Keep your eye on
gathering the funds
Take an active part in the process! Describe some specific steps towards your dream and with this involve more people and funds in your campaign.
Enjoy the result
So, the cherished sum is gathered! It is time to launch your dream!
